Onion Stir-Fry

Ready in just 10 minutes

    DIRECTIONS

  1. Mix soy sauce 2Tbs, starch syrup 1Tbs, and red pepper 1Tbs together.
  2. Slice onion to the desired thickness.
  3. Heat oil in a skillet and add onion.
  4. Stir onion for about 3 minutes.
  5. Add the sauce, green onions and cook for another 3 minutes.
  6. The food is ready to serve.

Korean Spicy Ramen

Are you ready to eat Korean style ramen?

    DIRECTIONS

  1. Pour 550 ml of water and bring to a boil.
  2. When the water boils, add flake and soup base.
  3. Add ramen noodles, egg and dumplings for better taste. Boil for 3 minutes.
  4. Add sliced green onions.
  5. Enjoy delicious Korean ramen!

Egg Salad Bagel

An easy and delicious way to make you happy.

    DIRECTIONS

  1. Boil, cool and peel eggs.
  2. Place eggs in a bowl and mash.
  3. Add mayonnaise, salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Place the bagel into the toaster oven for minutes.
  5. Cut the bagel in half.
  6. Put the egg salad on the bagel.
